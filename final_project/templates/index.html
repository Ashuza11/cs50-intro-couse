About Us

Founded the 20th December 2019. LEBC English Club stans for: Learning English For Better Communication English Club. Is a great platform for learners of all levels of English to find Authentic resources. LEBC English Club is a club at ULPGL Goma University in North KIVU, Goma twon, eastern regions of Democratic republique of the Congo(DRC). Learning English is so difficult for someone who doesn’t use authantic resources. That can depress him and push him to give up while starting to learn or at any level of the stage. To sum up the advantages, authentic materials:

  • Help prepare learners for the 'real' world of communication.
  • Guide learners toward the language they need for their particular context.
  • Motivate learners to communicate, because they help make communication real.

That is our Duty as an English club to help Learners have access to Authentic materials while they're about to learn English.
